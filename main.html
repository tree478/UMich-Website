Why is UMich the Best College To Go To?

If you want to go to an Ivy League college, but don’t have the money or you don’t think you will get in, University of Michigan is the perfect university for you! It is a lot cheaper than Ivy League colleges and has a higher acceptance rate, but it is just as great. It has an excellent athletic program if you are an athlete. If you are looking to go into medical, engineering, or software, then UMich has great programs for that too! It also has a very diverse student body, meaning that you will never have to worry about not finding people with similar interests and backgrounds! UMich is also one of the wealthier schools in America, which not only means you get bragging rights, but if you are a research major, this also means there are no financial limits to what you can research!

Housing/food

  • You can go the the Michigan Unions(Michigan League, Michigan Union, and Pierpont Commons), which are like the “living rooms” of the campus, where you can chill, eat, and hang out with friends
  • Michigan Dining has twelve on-campus cafes, seven markets, and seven dining halls that all serve delicious, healthy, gourmet food options, so you never have to worry about being hungry!
  • The on-campus dorms, Michigan Housing, offers not only dorms, but recreational areas and learning programs so you will never have to worry about being bored
    • 18 residence halls
    • 1480 apartments
    • Can accommodate undergrads, grads, and students with families
  • They also have a Beyond the Diagram program if you are looking for housing off-campus. You can find resources and there are student ambassadors who can connect you with off-campus housing options
  • Another really cool program is the Maize and Blue Cupboard, which is in place to make sure that everyone at UMich has access to healthy and nutritious food. You can get an MCard, through which, everything related to food is free! This means you can get free ingredients, kitchen and cookware, and even household supplies!

Job Opportunities

Weather

  • The weather at UMich is pretty temperate, not too hot or cold
  • In the summer, the high temperatures are usually around 80 degrees Fahrenheit
  • In the colder months, especially January and February, temperatures can drop to 15 degrees Fahrenheit

Tourist Attractions

  • There are a lot of theatres around, a very popular one being Michigan Theatre
  • Since dentistry is such a big thing at UMich, you should also go visit the Sindecuse Museum of Dentistry!
  • Or, if you are a history geek, go check out the University of Michigan Museum of Natural History!
  • If you are looking to go somewhere with your friends, Nickels Arcade is a great place to go!
  • And if you aren’t really sure about what you want to do or don’t have anything specific in mind, go walk around State Street! ?It has tons of shops and restaurants you can go visit!
